About Nduke Sunday

Hello! My name is Nduke Sunday, and I am a passionate tech enthusiast, educator, and aspiring backend software engineer. With over six years of teaching experience, I am dedicated to sharing knowledge and inspiring others, especially kids, to explore the exciting world of technology.

Professional Background

I specialize in backend development and have a strong interest in using Python, JavaScript, and modern web development frameworks. Currently, I am working on projects that involve Flask, JavaScript, and the MERN stack. One of my primary focuses is developing a Learning Management System (LMS) and a digital notary public platform.

Passion for Education

I am passionate about teaching kids coding and ICT concepts in a way that is engaging and visually intuitive. Through my company, NIOUN, I aim to empower kids aged 9-16 by offering lessons in programming, cybersecurity, and more.

ALX Journey
